Methods of Adoption:

  1. Domestic adoption: Adopting a child within the country.

  2. International adoption: Adopting a child from another country.

  3. Open adoption: Maintaining some level of contact with the birth parents.

  4. Closed adoption: No contact between adoptive and birth families.

  5. Kinship adoption: When a relative adopts a child (e.g., grandparents, aunts, uncles).
